🐱 How to Take Care of a Cat 🐾

🏠 Creating a Comfortable Home

Cats need a stable and enriched environment to thrive. Ensure they have:

  • A cozy resting area in a quiet place.
  • Scratching posts to maintain claws and avoid furniture damage.
  • Vertical spaces like cat trees to climb and feel safe.
  • Hiding spots such as covered beds or cardboard boxes.

🔗 International Cat Care – Understanding Cat Behavior

🍽️ Feeding Your Cat

Cats are obligate carnivores, meaning they need a diet rich in **animal protein**.

  • Recommended: Meat-based wet food for hydration.
  • Avoid: Dairy, raw fish, chocolate, onions, garlic, and grains.
  • Water intake: Cats tend to drink less, so hydration is crucial.

🔗 Cornell Feline Health Center – Cat Nutrition Guide

🚽 Litter Box Essentials

Maintaining a clean litter box is crucial for your cat’s health and hygiene.

  • Scoop daily to prevent odor and bacteria buildup.
  • One litter box per cat, plus one extra to prevent territorial issues.
  • Use unscented, clumping litter to encourage regular use.
  • Place the box in a quiet location, away from food and water.
